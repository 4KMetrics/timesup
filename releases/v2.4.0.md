# Release: Time's Up v2.4.0

**Release Date:** 2026-04-12
**Status:** Approved
**Manifest Version:** 3

## Release Summary

v2.4.0 adds a **Templates** section to settings so you can apply a curated blocklist + category preset with one click instead of typing out every site by hand. Useful when onboarding a new install or switching contexts (focus mode vs. casual browsing).

## New Features

### Templates Section with Diff Confirmation (FEAT-002)

- **Built-in templates.** A "Templates" section in settings offers curated presets (e.g. Social Media, Video & Streaming, News) that apply a set of blocklist entries, category groups, and recommended time limits.
- **Diff-before-apply.** Applying a template runs the same diff modal introduced in FEAT-001 (v2.3.0) — you see exactly which sites and limits will be added, removed, or changed before anything is committed to your config.
- **Non-destructive.** Templates merge with your existing config rather than replacing it. You can review and tweak before confirming.
